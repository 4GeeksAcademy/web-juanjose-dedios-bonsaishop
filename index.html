The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, and the pleasant exersice of effort and ingenuity for the grower.


By contrast with other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is not intented for production of food or for medicine. Instead, bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container
